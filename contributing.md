# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Awesome Jewelry Tech. This list is intentionally curated, not exhaustive. Every entry should clear a real bar of notability or differentiation.

## What belongs on this list

An entry should be at least one of:

- **Differentiated**: a meaningfully novel approach, capability, or category-defining product.
- **Industry-recognized**: widely adopted by working jewelers, gemologists, or manufacturers.
- **Significant team or funding**: backed by a serious team, institution, or capital.

The list is technology-focused. We include physical equipment, instruments, smart jewelry, and software. We do not include retailers, generic e-commerce shops, or unbranded supply.

## What does not belong

- Self-promotional listings without independent recognition.
- Duplicate or near-duplicate products of an already-listed entry.
- Outdated, abandoned, or unmaintained products.
- Generic categories or non-product resources (use the Communities & Resources section sparingly).
- Affiliate or referral links.

## How to add an entry

1. Fork the repository and create a branch.
2. Add your entry to the relevant category, keeping the section alphabetical.
3. Use the format: `- [Name](url) - One-sentence description.`
4. Descriptions must be in sentence case and end with a period.
5. Keep descriptions to a single line, ideally under 100 characters.
6. Do not use marketing language. Describe what the product is and why it matters.
7. Open a pull request with a brief justification of why the entry meets the bar.

## Adding a new category

If you believe a new top-level category is warranted, open an issue first to discuss before submitting a PR.

## Style

- Use the canonical product name and link to the official site when possible.
- Avoid emojis, badges, and stylistic flourishes inside entries.
- Keep formatting consistent with the rest of the list.

## Quality bar

Maintainers reserve the right to decline submissions that don't clear the curation bar, or to remove entries that no longer meet it.
