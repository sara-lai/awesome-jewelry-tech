# Contribution Guidelines

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Awesome Jewelry Tech. The list focuses on tools, equipment, software, or a type of technology with notability in the jewelry industry.

## How to add an entry

Easiest path: open an issue with your suggestion, or click the pencil (edit) icon on readme.md to propose the change directly. GitHub will walk you through the rest.

Format: `- [Name](url) - One-sentence description.` Single line, sentence case, period at the end. Aim for under 100 characters.

## Adding a new category

If a new top-level category seems warranted, open an issue first to discuss.

## Style

- Use the canonical product name and link to the official site when possible.
- Avoid emojis, badges, and marketing language.
- Keep formatting consistent with the rest of the list.
